Planet Data

This page lets you get a more detailed overview of the closest 20 planets in the local system. Positive values represent a surplus in materials and Negative values represent a desire for materials. The price data matches this accordingly, showing either a buy or sell price based on the planets current material usage.

View your next planet!

Once your done looking at the information on the current planet, switch to the next using the button below and the data will be updated with the new planets current state. you can cycle through all 20 planets this way.
